It's hard to find good media that helps you grow, in this moment. (Rather than simply entertain)

Here's a non-exhaustive list:

If you think I've missed anything please email me.

Actionable Life Advice

  • Newel of Knowledge - Phenomenal, ~20 minute, lectures (+whiteboard) on social interaction and how bad habits are ultimately a result of underlying unmet psychological needs.
  • Vlogbrothers - Excellent, ~3 minute, spontaneous discussions about large life questions.
  • Goobie and Doobie - Brillliant, long-form, ~1 hour, monologue lectures about health, nutrition, the systems we live within and the future of humanity.

Mindfulness

  • Waking Up - Neuroscientist Sam Harris (and various other speakers) help you learn how to focus your attention, practice (insight) meditation, and live a more present life.

French

The best french podcast ever.

Each episode, ~20 minutes, is on a new and interesting topic.

She introduces the new vocabulary briefly in english and then continues her discussion, so you actually learn the content. At the end of each episode she reads her script again but at regular speed.
