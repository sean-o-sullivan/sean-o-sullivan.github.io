7-Segment Mechanical Clock

I created this clock for our school canteen. The time is indicated by the servo-arm positions.

Description

I have worked on this project for the last two years (part-time). Instead of hiding the wires, I chose to display them, creating this flowing, visual effect.


This clock was based on work from Michael Klements at the DIY-Life. I designed the laser-cut backing board, modified the software for moving the segments between time changes and accounted for Irish daylight savings. The segments are made from neon-green PETG, spray-painted black.

In the ten days before Christmas, the clock now displays a countdown.
